So What is Hello World Hack?

If you've never attended a hackathon, or have just picked up programming
and want to get a project off the ground, this is the perfect hackathon for you!
The Hello World Hackathon is a hackathon created for beginners.
Filled with workshops on setting up a project, building it, and testing it.
Awesome prizes and food galore! The best part, it's all free!
